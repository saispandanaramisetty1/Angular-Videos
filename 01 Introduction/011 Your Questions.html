As you go through the course, you may have questions or something may not work as you expect. So, here are a few of tips for you: 


1- Do your research first. This will make you a better and more independent developer.

I can guarantee that in more than 90% of the courses, you can simply type your question or the error message you get in Google and you'll find the solution in less than 10 minutes! 

If you can't find your answer, post your question on the discussion board. 


2- Help each other. Answering questions help you better learn the materials in the course. 

So, If you have any spare time, look at the unanswered questions and help your classmates.
